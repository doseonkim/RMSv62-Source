RadianceMS

What is RadianceMS, you might ask? RadianceMS is a v62 MapleStory Private Server. There are many things you couldn't even think about on Global MS or any other kind. You can be more than one job having all the skills, looks, items, mesos, power and everything you've dreamed about doing on MapleStory! There are many Events going on during the day increasing the fun of the server!

We highly suggest you sign up and use the forums. The forums will help you very much for your questions and suggestions for the server. You can also just ask people in game, but if you are able to and are having difficulties, the forum is the way to go!
